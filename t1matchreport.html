IPL 2024 Match Reports

Qualifiers 1: KKR vs SRH

Date: May 21, 2024

Venue: Narendra Modi Stadium, Ahmedabad

KKR started the IPL 2024 season with a bang by defeating SRH by 8 wickets(38 balls left). The match witnessed stellar performances from Mitchell Starc and Sunil Narine, who guided their team to victory.

Eliminator: RR vs RCB

Date: May 22, 2024

Venue: Narendra Modi Stadium, Ahmedabad

RR vs RCB IPL 2024 Eliminator Highlights: Rajasthan Royals beat Royal Challengers Bengaluru by 4 wickets to advance to the Qualifier 2.

Qualifiers 2: RR vs SRH

Date: May 24, 2024

Venue: MA Chidambaram Stadium, Chennai

Sunrisers Hyderabad vs Rajasthan Royals IPL 2024 Highlights: Sunrisers Hyderabad recorded a resounding a 36-run win over Rajasthan Royals to book their third IPL final during the Qualifier 2 match at the MA Chidambaram Stadium in Chennai.

Finals: KKR vs SRH

Date: May 26, 2024

Venue: MA Chidambaram Stadium, Chennai

Kolkata Knight Riders beat Sunrisers Hyderabad by 8 wickets. And won 2024 IPL Championship.SRH stood as Runner Up They Performed Well Throughout Ipl Season.
