Chennai Super Kings defeated Gujarat Titans in the final of IPL 2023 to lift a record-equalling fifth trophy.

2022 - Gujarat Titans

Under the leadership of Hardik Pandya, GT defeated Rajasthan Royals in the IPL 2022 finals by seven wickets to claim their inaugural championship title.

2021 - Chennai Super Kings

Chennai Super Kings won the match by 27 runs beating Kolkata Knight Riders, to win their fourth IPL title.
